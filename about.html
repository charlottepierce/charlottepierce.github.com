What's all this, then?

My name is Charlotte Pierce, and I'm a PhD candidate in computer science at Swinburne University of Technology in Melbourne, Australia.

My major research interests lie in the intersection of music and technology, whether that be through teaching tools, computational analysis, assisting creative endeavours, or enhancing the listening experience.

This is mostly a place for me to put up random bits of work and notes on whatever happens to spark my enthusiasm. If it helps anybody else, great. If not, at least I can enjoy the false sense of productivity.

I have both a GitHub and Bitbucket account, and occasionally post work on SoundCloud.
